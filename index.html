Beyond Barriers Tutoring

Welcome to Beyond Barriers Tutoring, where we break down barriers together one lesson at a time! Join us today for low cost, compassionate tutoring, with pricing based on client need. We will work with anyone, but will have lower rates for some.

Our tutors are well trained in working with adverse youth and adults, be it those with learning disabilities or that are low-income. We are here to find the best way for you to learn, and support you on your journey to academic success, without draining your wallet!

Rates

Discounted Individuals $15-22/hour
Standard Based on Difficulty $20-30/hour

Our rates are determined at our discretion, based on our understanding of your needs.
Note: We are currently only offering services online, with rare exceptions, as we build clientele and staff. Thank you for understanding!

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a tailored learning experience to those who could benefit from it the most. We are compassion and equity driven individuals that want to see disadvantaged peoples flourish!

Our sliding-scale rates provide us the funding to offer the lowest prices possible to those who need it most! Choosing Beyond Barriers Tutoring, even at the highest rates, benefits someone else in need. Join us today!
